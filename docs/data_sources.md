## Why This Dataset Was Selected

This dataset supports a revenue integrity analytics story because it contains provider, specialty, HCPCS service, utilization, submitted charge, allowed amount, Medicare payment, and standardized payment fields.

It allows analysis of reimbursement variation, service utilization patterns, provider outliers, and coding/payment risk indicators.

## Important Framing

This project does not claim to identify fraud or improper billing. Public aggregate Medicare data can only support risk indicators and audit prioritization.
